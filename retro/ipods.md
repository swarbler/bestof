# iPods

## Easy to Mod

- iPod Classic 5th generation *(5G)* or 5.5 Enhanced *(5.5G)*
- iPod Mini

## Difficult to Mod

- iPod Classic 6th or 7th generation *(hard to open)*

## Impractical to Mod

- iPod Nano *(can replace battery with good soldering, but cannot upgrade storage)*
- iPod Touch
